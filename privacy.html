Privacy Policy – Slime King Merge

Last updated: April 2026

Slime King Merge (“the Game”) is developed by SBG Games (“we”, “our”, or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how the Game collects, uses, and protects user data.

1. Information We Collect

The Game itself does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, third-party advertising services used in the Game may collect certain device-level and usage-related data.

2. Third-Party Advertising Services

2.1 Unity Ads

The Game uses Unity Ads to display interstitial advertisements.

Unity Ads may collect and process data such as:

  • Advertising ID / device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Approximate region or country
  • Ad interaction and performance data

Purpose of this data:

  • Displaying ads
  • Ad personalization (where applicable)
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Measuring ad performance

For more information, please review Unity’s privacy documentation:

Unity Game Player and App User Privacy Policy
Unity Ads – Google Play Data Safety Information

3. How We Use Data

We use third-party advertising services only to:

  • Show interstitial ads in the Game
  • Improve ad delivery and performance
  • Maintain the functionality and sustainability of the Game

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Collect your name, email address, or phone number directly through the Game
  • Collect precise GPS location

4. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not directed specifically to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children directly. However, third-party advertising providers may process limited device data as described above.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the Game and its integrations. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
